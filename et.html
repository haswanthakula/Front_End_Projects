A World Away from Home

The abrupt transition from the carefree days at home to the disciplined environment of a hostel at fourteen was a shock to my system. Overwhelmed by homesickness, I spent countless nights crying, yearning for the familiar comforts and unwavering love of my family. Despite the initial turmoil, the hostel gradually transformed into a crucible of character-building. In an era devoid of smartphones and constant digital distractions, we forged deep connections, sharing laughter, secrets, and dreams. These unfiltered bonds, formed under the vast expanse of the night sky, are treasures I hold close to my heart.



Milestone Achieved

The day my 10th class results were announced stands out as a pinnacle moment in my life. Achieving perfect scores was a dream come true, but witnessing my parents' overwhelming joy and pride made the experience even more extraordinary. Their tears of happiness were a testament to their unwavering support and belief in me. In that moment, I felt an immense sense of fulfillment and wished time could stand still, preserving the pure joy and love that surrounded us.

Expanding the Family

Turning sixteen was a milestone, but my brother's wedding completely redefined life. It was a whirlwind of emotions - excitement, nervousness, and a touch of melancholy as I watched my brother embark on a new journey. The wedding was a grand affair, filled with laughter, tears, and unforgettable moments. Dancing the night away with friends and family created memories I cherish. Most importantly, I gained a new member in our family, a sister-in-law who brought a fresh perspective and warmth to our home.



The Great Hostel Heist

Hostel life was a rollercoaster of experiences. One unforgettable night involved a daring escapade to retrieve a stolen mobile phone from a fellow student's cabin. The thrill of the operation was matched only by our relief when we successfully recovered the device. Thanks to the magic of Jio, we even managed to squeeze some internet usage out of the old keypad phone. Those late nights spent watching movies together, huddled in our rooms, created a bond that time couldn't erase. It was a period marked by youthful exuberance, camaraderie, and the joy of shared adventures.

A Hero's Wake-Up Call

The allure of the silver screen, combined with an unwavering devotion to my favorite hero, Pawan Kalyan, led to some early morning adventures. Stealthily slipping out of bed before dawn, I would venture to the nearest theater, heart pounding with excitement. The solitary experience of immersing myself in a Pawan Kalyan movie, bathed in the soft glow of the screen, was pure bliss. Returning home with the first rays of sunlight, I would concoct elaborate tales of early morning classes to cover my tracks. Those clandestine escapades remain cherished memories, a testament to the power of cinema and the thrill of youthful rebellion.

Bunk and Bliss

The first year of B.Tech was a wild ride of newfound freedom and blossoming friendships. Gone were the rigid schedules of school, replaced by a sense of independence that sometimes bordered on...well, rebellion. Attending classes became a game of chance, with entire days occasionally sacrificed at the altar of movie marathons.Those were the days! Afternoon classes were often sacrificed in favor of more enticing pursuits. With a shared mischievous glint, my friends and I would orchestrate elaborate plans to ditch lectures. Our hostel room transformed into a makeshift cinema, complete with smuggled snacks and borrowed laptops. Hours would melt away as we lost ourselves in the world of movies, creating a universe of our own, far removed from the academic grind.



Industrial Visit Extravaganza

The industrial tour to Vizag was an unforgettable adventure. Our college bus journey was filled with anticipation as we explored the industrial landscape. The night held even more excitement as we checked into a hotel. The evening was a whirlwind of fun, with electrifying dance performances keeping us entertained well into the night. The camaraderie and laughter shared with friends created a bond that would last a lifetime. It was a perfect blend of education and entertainment, a memory etched in gold.

A Leap of Faith

The sting of rejection after college placements ignited a spark within me. Determined to carve my own path, I made the bold decision to relocate to Hyderabad, a city teeming with opportunities. It was a leap of faith, leaving behind the familiar comforts of home to embrace the unknown. Armed with ambition and a suitcase of dreams, I embarked on a new chapter, eager to learn, grow, and discover my potential.

Behind the Wheel with Brothers

Learning to drive was an unforgettable adventure, thanks to my brothers. With our dad's old car as our training ground, we embarked on countless road trips. Covering distances of 50 to 60 kilometers, we spent entire days mastering the art of driving. From early morning to late night, the open road became our classroom. Those shared experiences, filled with laughter, sibling rivalry, and the thrill of independence, forged a bond that would last a lifetime.
